About Me

I am a faculty member at IIIT Ranchi who completed my doctoral studies under the esteemed guidance of Professor Balasubramanian Raman in the Computer Science and Engineering department at IIT Roorkee. My research focuses on Medical Imaging, Image Processing, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and AI.

My academic journey has been marked by numerous prestigious awards and scholarships, including the Prathibha award and merit scholarships from the Andhra Pradesh government. My research has received funding from esteemed institutions such as the Ministry of India, DORA IITR, and Microsoft Research.

Research Areas

Medical Imaging

Specializing in advanced medical image analysis, segmentation, and classification using deep learning approaches.

Computer Vision

Developing innovative solutions for image processing, pattern recognition, and visual understanding.

Machine Learning & AI

Exploring cutting-edge machine learning techniques with applications in healthcare and computer vision.
